## ğŸ¯ Why This Framework Works

Traditional landing pages focus on features ("We have AI!") or vague benefits ("Transform your business!"). 

This approach works because it:

1. **Starts with empathy** - "I understand your frustration"
2. **Provides clarity** - "Here's exactly how we solve it"
3. **Paints aspiration** - "Imagine life without that problem"
4. **Removes friction** - "Take this obvious, low-risk next step"
5. **Uses customer language** - Words they'd actually say

The structure follows the proven **Problem-Agitate-Solution** framework with clear benefit statements and a strong call to action.
